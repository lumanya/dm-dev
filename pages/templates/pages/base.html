About Us

In partnership with the Tanzania Media Foundation (TMF), Afyatoon is initiating a digital Collaborative story ideation platform to link journalists to health experts to facilitate AMR based story collaboration.

As a journalist or student of journalism, this platform will give you collaborators for your publications ranging from online content, newspaper articles to scripts for documentaries and commentaries around health-related topics with an initial focus on antimicrobial resistance. These collaborators are renowned health experts under the Afyatoon team and beyond.

As a health expert, thgis platform will provide you will a tool to communicate your scientific findings or finding from other scientist around different health related topics with an initial focus on antimicrobial resistance to the rest of the Tanzanian community through students of journalism or journalists practicing in Tanzania.

Regardless of whether you are a health expert or Journalist, all you need to do is register onto the platform and start your writing journey towards collaborative story ideation with others. We at Afyatoon will help you moderate the link.
